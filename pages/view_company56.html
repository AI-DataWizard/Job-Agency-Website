CityMove Transit

Chicago, Illinois

about company

CityMove Transit provides innovative urban mobility solutions including micro-transit services, corporate shuttle programs, and last-mile connectivity in metropolitan areas.

We reimagine urban transportation through flexible routing, modern fleets, and technology-driven solutions that reduce congestion and improve commuter experiences.

  • Established: 2018
  • Employees: 220+ drivers, dispatchers, and mobility specialists
  • Fleet: 150+ eco-friendly vehicles including electric shuttles and hybrid vans
  • Services: Corporate Shuttles, Airport Transfers, Event Transportation, First/Last Mile Solutions
  • Technology: Real-time tracking app with dynamic routing and booking system
