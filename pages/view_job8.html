CurePlus Pharmacy seeks a detail-oriented Pharmacy Technician to support our busy retail pharmacy in Pretoria. You'll assist pharmacists with dispensing medications, managing inventory, processing medical aid claims, and providing excellent customer service. This role requires precision, organizational skills, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment while maintaining strict compliance standards.
