about company

Justice Partners Inc. is a public interest law firm dedicated to civil rights, social justice litigation, and advocacy for marginalized communities and systemic reform.

We fight for equity and justice through strategic litigation, policy advocacy, and community empowerment initiatives that challenge injustice and create meaningful change.

  • Established: 2005
  • Employees: 45+ attorneys, paralegals, and advocacy specialists
  • Focus Areas: Civil Rights, Housing Justice, Immigration, Criminal Justice Reform
  • Cases Handled: 300+ precedent-setting lawsuits
  • Funding: Non-profit structure supported by grants and donations
